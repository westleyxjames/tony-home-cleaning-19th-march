About TrueShine Cleaning

We're not just a cleaning service—we're your partner in maintaining a clean, healthy, and stress-free home.

Our Story

TrueShine Cleaning is a trusted residential cleaning service dedicated to delivering high-quality results with attention to detail. Founded in Portland, Oregon, we've built our reputation on reliability, professionalism, and exceptional customer service.

We understand that your home is your sanctuary, and keeping it clean shouldn't be stressful. That's why we've assembled a team of experienced, trained professionals who treat every home with the care and respect it deserves.

From standard cleaning to deep cleans and move-out services, we bring the same level of dedication and quality to every job, big or small.

Our Mission

To make homes cleaner, healthier, and stress-free by providing exceptional cleaning services that exceed expectations and build lasting relationships with our customers.
